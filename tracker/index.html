Five Dimensions

What this tracker measures that no other does.

DIMENSION 01
Leader Psychology
Narcissism, impulsivity, values anchor, survival incentive, accountability exposure — with formal weightings. Survival incentive is 25% weight — highest — because loss aversion research is clear: leaders fighting for personal survival take disproportionate risks.
DIMENSION 02
Factuality Layer
The gap between what leaders say publicly and what the verifiable record shows. That gap is not a communication error. It is where the actual strategy lives.
DIMENSION 03
Structural Guardrails
What remains of the systems designed to create friction before catastrophic decisions. Congressional constraint 20/100. Diplomatic channels 22/100. Arms control 15/100.
DIMENSION 04
Escalation Signals
Nuclear signalling, cyber intensity, proxy network activity. The tells analysts miss when they are only watching hardware.
DIMENSION 05
Ideological Philosophy
What a leader cannot see is more dangerous than what a leader will not do. When ideology dehumanises the adversary it degrades intelligence. Not a moral variable. A strategic one.
About This Tracker

I was done with sleepless nights about outcomes decided by people I would not trust to house sit my cats.

Not because the situation is not serious. It is. But because the anxiety was coming from treating these conflicts as forces of nature when they are being decided by a small number of specific human beings.

Why It Exists

We are told these conflicts are too complex to understand. Too many variables. Too much history. We feel what we are supposed to feel: overwhelmed, anxious, and ultimately passive.

I am Pakistani. I have lived inside the kind of geopolitical decisions that get made in rooms I am not in, about people who look like me, by people who have never met anyone who looks like me. 2001. 2003. A direct line from each of those decisions to the current Iran war.

3 trillion dollars. 500,000 deaths. All of it predictable — not from the intelligence, but from the psychology of the people interpreting it.

The Purpose
"We cannot just be consumers of smoke and screen. We have a responsibility to distil and share information. To help clear the haze."

This tracker started as a personal discipline. Not to tell anyone what will happen. To force every assessment through a factual filter before it becomes a conclusion. Because a prediction without a proof base is just a fear with a narrative attached.

Leadership Psychology

Why the human variable
determines every other variable.

The theoretical basis for why leader psychology is a strategic input, not a biographical footnote.

Theoretical Foundation

Prospect Theory (Kahneman and Tversky, 1979): Leaders make decisions based on perceived losses relative to their personal reference point. Netanyahu at 96/100 survival incentive is not making the same calculation as a leader at 30/100.

Narcissistic Leadership (Rosenthal and Pittinsky, 2006): Narcissistic leaders systematically overestimate their capabilities, extend conflicts past rational endpoints, and only exit when they can frame it as a personal victory.

Loss Aversion in Conflict (McDermott, 2004): Leaders facing personal loss take disproportionate risks. Three active corruption trials suspended under wartime emergency is a loss aversion variable producing specific predictable behaviour.

The Role of Ideological Philosophy

The five dimensions measure what a leader will and will not do. Ideological philosophy measures what a leader is unable to see.

When an ideological framework dehumanises an entire group, it produces a cognitive filter. The leader stops receiving accurate intelligence about that group. The result is not evil strategy. It is degraded strategy.

"What a leader is unwilling to see is more dangerous than what a leader is unwilling to do."

Nazi Germany lost the nuclear race not because of military failure but because antisemitism drove out the scientists who could have won it. October 7 was the most significant intelligence failure in Israeli history — under the most intensive surveillance architecture in the world. The mechanism is identical.

Assessment Watch

When to update.
What triggered the flag.

Two sections. Leadership psychology is auto-monitored per leader, per dimension. Overall risk variables are static by design — requiring human judgment, not just code.

Section 1 — Leadership Psychology Assessment
Per-leader, per-dimension monitoring. Each dimension is watched against its formal weight — survival (25%) flags at ±2.0 pts, accountability (22%) at ±2.3 pts, narcissism (20%) at ±2.5 pts, impulsivity (18%) at ±2.8 pts, values (15%) at ±3.0 pts. Higher weight = lower threshold. A flag here means GDELT has detected news volume, tone, and corroboration consistent with a material change in how that dimension should score for that leader. Review the evidence. If it holds, update leaders.json and commit.
Section 2 — Overall Risk Assessment Variables
These variables are static by design — they do not update automatically. This is intentional and the reason matters.
"Code can detect volume and tone. It cannot detect meaning. Whether the Oman talks represent a genuine diplomatic breakthrough or a stalling tactic while missiles are repositioned is a judgment call. That judgment belongs to a human."

Variables like Congressional Constraint (20/100), Diplomatic Channels (22/100), and Arms Control Architecture (15/100) represent structural conditions — the presence or absence of institutional frameworks designed to create friction before catastrophic decisions. These cannot be derived from headline count or GDELT tone alone. A ceasefire announcement that collapses in 48 hours looks identical in raw data to a genuine negotiated pause.

The dimension weightings that govern these variables — how much each one contributes to the escalation probability calculation — are validated against real-time developments. When guardrails all fall below 25/100 simultaneously (as they do now), individual psychology becomes the primary determinant of outcomes. That threshold is not arbitrary. It is calibrated against the seven historical case studies in the Proof of Concept, and it is updated when evidence warrants. But the update itself requires human review, source citation, and a confidence rating. The agent flags. You decide.

How to update: When a variable changes materially — a ceasefire holds for 72+ hours, War Powers passes, a new arms control framework is announced — update the value in the dashboard sliders or directly in the source JSON. Mark reviewed below. The change is logged with your timestamp.

Section 3 — Dimension Proposals
Proposals generated by cumulative analysis of the last 30 days of verified news. Each proposal requires cross-corroborated evidence across multiple source blocs before it appears here. Nothing updates leaders.json without your approval. Review the evidence chain. Approve only if it holds.
